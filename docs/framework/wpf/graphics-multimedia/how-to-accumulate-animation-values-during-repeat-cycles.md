<span data-ttu-id="56017-103">Este ejemplo muestra cómo utilizar el <xref:System.Windows.Media.Animation.DoubleAnimation.IsCumulative%2A> propiedad que se va a acumular valores de animaciones entre ciclos que se repiten.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="56017-103">This example shows how to use the <xref:System.Windows.Media.Animation.DoubleAnimation.IsCumulative%2A> property to accumulate animation values across repeating cycles.</span></span>

 <span data-ttu-id="56017-105">Use la <xref:System.Windows.Media.Animation.DoubleAnimation.IsCumulative%2A> propiedad que se va a acumular los valores base de una animación entre ciclos que se repiten.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="56017-105">Use the <xref:System.Windows.Media.Animation.DoubleAnimation.IsCumulative%2A> property to accumulate base values of an animation across repeating cycles.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="56017-106">Por ejemplo, si establece una animación se repita 9 veces (<xref:System.Windows.Media.Animation.Timeline.RepeatBehavior%2A> = "9 x") y establezca la propiedad que se va a animar entre 10 y 15 (From = 10 To = 15), la propiedad se anima de 10 a 15 durante el primer ciclo, de 15 a 20 durante el segundo ciclo , de 20 a 25 durante el tercer ciclo y así sucesivamente.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="56017-106">For example, if you set an animation to repeat 9 times (<xref:System.Windows.Media.Animation.Timeline.RepeatBehavior%2A> = "9x") and you set the property to animate between 10 and 15 (From = 10 To = 15), the property animates from 10 to 15 during the first cycle, from 15 to 20 during the second cycle, from 20 to 25 during the third cycle, and so on.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="56017-107">Por lo tanto, cada ciclo de animación usa el valor de animación final del ciclo de animación anterior como su valor base.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="56017-107">Hence, each animation cycle uses the ending animation value from the previous animation cycle as its base value.</span></span>
